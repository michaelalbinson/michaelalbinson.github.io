CIRQUE 2019 is proud to announce our partnership with Scotiabank - our title sponsor! Each year, Scotiabank sends delegates to CIRQUE to talk about financial technologies and how engineering degrees from all disciplines are welcome to help solve complex problems from a variety of different fields.

Every year, Scotiabank has hired delegates on for internships and full-time positions, and they are looking to hire through our conference again this year! Join us to learn more and network with these industry professionals!
