Partners of CIRQUE 2019

A huge thank you to all the companies that sponsored CIRQUE 2019! A special thank you to our Diamond, Gold, Silver, and Bronze level sponsors: Scotiabank, StarFish Medical, Bidell Gas Compression, TRANE and Athabasca Oil Corporation!

Scotiabank

Our Title Sponsor!

Operating as Scotiabank, the Bank of Nova Scotia is a Canadian multinational bank and the third largest bank in Canada in terms of deposits and market capital. The company provides services and products ranging from personal and commercial banking to corporate and investment banking for over 24 million customers across almost 50 countries. As “Canada’s most international bank,” Scotiabank is among the world leaders in banking and provides opportunities for millions to grow financially.

StarFish Medical

StarFish Medical is Canada’s largest full service medical device design company. The company offers award winning design, development, and manufacturing services based in Toronto and Victoria. A Growth 500 2018 ranked company, StarFish specializes in complex electromechanical medical devices. Their Pathfinder™ process is used to reduce wasted effort and increase success for medical device product definition, technical engineering, and product development. StarFish manufactures prototype and low volume medical devices within an ISO 13485 certified Quality Management System and an FDA registered manufacturing and clean room facility.

Bidell Gas Compression

Bidell Gas Compression, a subsidiary of Total Energy Services Inc., is a leading supplier and service provider of reciprocating and rotary screw natural gas compressors. The company is based in Calgary, Canada with parts and service centers in Western Canada. Bidell’s in-house engineering, design, and retrofit teams are experienced in all facets of natural gas compression and are committed to exceeding customer expectations and industry standards. Bidell is a full and certified provider of OEM equipment including Caterpillar, Waukesha, Ariel and Sullair. Their patented NOMAD trailer mounted compressor eliminates many of the traditional drawbacks of skid mounted packages and delivers unprecedented value and performance to their customers. Now exceeding 1,000,000 field operating hours, the NOMAD is revolutionizing how the industry utilizes its gas compression assets.

TRANE

At Ingersoll Rand we are passionate about inspiring progress around the world.  We advance the quality of life by creating comfortable, sustainable and efficient environments. Our people and our family of brands—including Club Car®, Ingersoll Rand®, Thermo King®, Trane®, American Standard® Heating & Air Conditioning and ARO® - work together to enhance the quality and comfort of air in homes and buildings; transport and protect food and perishables; and increase industrial productivity and efficiency. We are a global business committed to a world of sustainable progress and enduring results. For more information, visit http://www.ingersollrand.com/.  

Within TRANE we are recruiting for Sales Engineers. As a Sales Engineer you work with Engineers, Architects, Contractors and owners/developers in the design, application and sale of systems, controls, parts and services for commercial, industrial and institutional buildings. These systems will include the world's most complete line of commercial, industrial and institutional Heating, Ventilating and Air Conditioning (HVAC) equipment and Building Management Controls. We offer full time and internship opportunities. Please visit us at www.ingersollrand/careers to apply.

Callidus Engineering

Callidus Engineering is a professional engineering firm with offices located in London and Kingston, ON. The company provides mechanical and electrical engineering services to an ever-expanding group of clients in Southwestern and Eastern Ontario. Their mission is to provide an excellent engineering product coupled with exceptional customer service. The company goal is to develop and maintain a strong profile in the mechanical and electrical consulting engineering field. To this end, Callidus is committed to the growth and development of their client base and to the personal growth and development of their staff.

Athabasca Oil Corporation

Athabasca Oil Corporation (AOC) is an intermediate oil-weighted producer with exposure to several of the largest resource plays in Western Canada including the Montney, Duvernay and Oil Sands. The company is focused on the sustainable development of light oil resources in northwestern Alberta and oil sands in the Athabasca region. Over the first half of 2017, AOC has closed a transformational acquisition of thermal oil assets, continued to actively drill and expand their light oil assets, and completed a substantial balance sheet refinancing. The company is positioned for sustainable cash flow generation and a fully funded development outlook capable of delivering growth to 60,000 boe/d by 2020. AOC is looking to continue its growth strategy into the future and offers an exciting and entrepreneurial work environment for the right candidate.
