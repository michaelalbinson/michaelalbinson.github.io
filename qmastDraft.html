QMAST - The Queen's Mostly Autonomous Sailboat Team

MAST is a team that designs, builds, programs and tests robotic sailboats. We compete in a variety of competitions like Sailbot, and strive to create robust sailboats that are fully autonomous. We welcome all engineers, especially those interested in navel architecture, integrated electrical systems or software for robots.
