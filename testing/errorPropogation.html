Enter Your Equation




quick hints:

  • make sure that there is only one variable on the left side of the equation
  • know that only standard operators are allowed (* / - +)
  • some standard functions and values are allowed (PI, E, log(), ln())
  • note that PI and E are RESERVED KEYS (for now), so you cannot include 'PI' or 'E' in any variable names, or they will be removed
  • exponents are allowed, but make sure they are separated from the rest of the equation by a space (i.e. e^2 + 2 will be evaluated as (e^2) + 2, but e^2+2 will be evaluated as e^4)
